Hello, and welcome to Double Treble Guitar Shop. Established in 1983, right here in Richmond, KY. We are a small, family run shop that specializes in selling and repairing guitars. Our first and foremost goal is customer service in whatever way necessary, as has been since we opened. We accept walk-in repairs, as well as trade-in guitars for resale. We have a full inventory of various guitars of multiple brands from around the world, for a player of every skill level and background. If you sign up to become a member, you can get access to our Private Reserve of highly valuable, collectable guitars to look at, play, or even take home. Please feel free to contact us and we can discuss your needs. Give us a call at 1-800-555-7777.
